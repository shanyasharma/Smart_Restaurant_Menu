Gujarati Desserts

Basundi

Soft and spongy cottage cheese (paneer) balls cooked in sugar syrup.

Price: ₹120


Gulab Jamun

A rice pudding made with milk, rice, sugar, and flavored with cardamom, saffron, and nuts.

Price: ₹200


Kaju Katli

Deep-fried batter soaked in sugar syrup, forming a crispy and sweet spiral.

Price: ₹10


Malpua

Soft paneer (cottage cheese) dumplings soaked in sweetened, thickened milk, flavored with cardamom and saffron.

Price: ₹200


Shrikhand

A sweet pudding-like dessert made with semolina, flour, or carrots, cooked in ghee (clarified butter) with sugar and flavored with cardamom and nuts.

Price: ₹150
